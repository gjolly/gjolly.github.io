About me

I started studying Computer Engineering in France before moving to Switzerland. I am currenty studying at EPFL as an exchange student.

Since I have always liked to create things by myself and understanding the world around me, I have done scientific studies. When came the time to chose a study field I obviously chose Computer Science. In fact, hacking computers had always been a game for me.

I went to ENSEEIHT where I learned the basics of computer networks, the Internet and professional development.

At EPFL I have focused my studies on distributed systems to link system development and networks. I have also taken courses on data visulisation to stay up to date with the last JavaScript evolutions.

I have worked on telecommunications systems, decentralized systems and web services. I like to learn new things on every project I realise.
